About Arcflow

Finance built for builders

We started Arcflow because we saw how many good businesses were held back by poor financial visibility. We're here to change that.

We believe finance should enable growth, not just report on it

Most growing businesses operate in a blind spot — their books are a few months behind, their MIS is a spreadsheet nobody trusts, and their CFO is someone they can't yet afford. Meanwhile, real decisions get made on gut feel.

Arcflow exists to close that gap. We bring the financial infrastructure of a mature company to businesses that are still finding their footing — without the full-time headcount cost.

We work entirely remotely, which means we can serve clients across India and internationally, without the overheads of a traditional firm. That efficiency translates directly into better value for you.

Whether you're a founder managing your first few crores or a mid-sized business preparing for your next round — we meet you where you are and build with you from there.

Accuracy over speed

We never cut corners on numbers. Every figure we deliver has been checked, reconciled, and verified.

Proactive, not reactive

We flag issues before they become problems. That's the difference between a vendor and a partner.

Honest on price

No surprise fees, no scope creep without discussion. What we quote is what you pay.

What makes us different

  • Senior professionals on every engagement — not interns or associates
  • We understand business, not just accounting
  • Flexible scope — scale up or down as you need
  • No long-term contracts required to get started
  • Fast onboarding — typically live within 48 hours
  • Comfortable with Indian and international reporting standards

Who we work with

  • Early-stage startups that have outgrown a CA-on-call
  • Growth-stage companies preparing for a fundraise
  • SMEs that want better financial visibility
  • International businesses with India operations
  • Founders who want a finance partner, not just a service provider
