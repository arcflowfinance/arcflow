What We Do

Finance operations, end to end

We cover every layer of your finance function — from day-to-day bookkeeping to CFO-level strategy. All remote, all on-demand.

01

Bookkeeping

Clean books are the foundation of every financial decision. We make sure yours are always up to date and audit-ready.

Monthly Reconciliations

Bank, credit card, and wallet reconciliations done every month — no backlogs, no surprises. Every transaction accounted for.

P&L and Balance Sheet

Accurate monthly financial statements that give you a real picture of where your business stands — not just what happened last quarter.

Accounts Payable & Receivable

We track what you owe and what you're owed, flag overdue invoices, and help you stay on top of working capital.

Payroll Accounting

Payroll entries, salary registers, and statutory deductions recorded accurately every cycle.

02

MIS Reporting

Dashboards and reports that translate financial data into decisions. Built for founders, not accountants.

Custom Dashboard Design

We build dashboards tailored to your business model — whether you're tracking ARR, unit economics, or department-level costs.

Weekly & Monthly Reports

Structured reporting on a cadence that suits you — delivered on time, every time, in a format your leadership team can actually use.

KPI Tracking & Variance Analysis

We define the metrics that matter, track actuals vs budget, and flag where things are drifting — before they become a problem.

Investor-Ready Reporting

If you have investors or a board, we format your MIS to the standard they expect — clean, comparable, and commentary-led.

03

FP&A

Financial planning that connects your numbers to your strategy. Built for decisions, not just compliance.

Annual Budgets & Forecasts

We work with you to build a realistic, driver-based annual plan — grounded in your business model and stress-tested for key assumptions.

Scenario Modelling

What happens if revenue drops 20%? What if you hire 10 people next quarter? We model the scenarios that keep you up at night.

Rolling Forecasts

Static annual plans go stale. We maintain a rolling 12-month forecast that updates as your business evolves.

Board & Investor Presentations

We translate your financials into board-ready decks — clear narrative, sharp numbers, zero jargon.

04

Fractional CFO

Senior CFO-level thinking, available on your terms. For businesses that need strategic finance leadership without a full-time hire.

Fundraise & Investor Support

From building your financial model to preparing for due diligence — we help you walk into investor conversations with confidence.

Cash Flow Strategy

We monitor your cash position, identify pressure points early, and build strategies to maintain healthy liquidity through every growth phase.

Finance Team Setup & Oversight

Building an in-house finance team? We help you hire right, set up processes, and provide oversight while the team gets up to speed.

Strategic Decision Support

Pricing changes, new market entry, M&A, ESOP structuring — we bring CFO-level rigour to your most important financial decisions.

05

Tax Filing

Timely, accurate, fully compliant. We handle your tax obligations so you never miss a deadline or pay an unnecessary penalty.

GST Returns

GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, annual returns — filed on time, every time. We also handle GST reconciliations and notices if they arise.

TDS Compliance

TDS deduction, deposit, and return filing (24Q, 26Q) managed end-to-end. No defaults, no interest payments.

Income Tax Returns

Corporate and individual ITR filing, with advance tax computation and planning to minimise your tax outflow legally.

Tax Planning & Advisory

Proactive tax planning — not reactive filing. We identify deductions, exemptions, and structures that work in your favour.
